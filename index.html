Hyperliquid Staking Overview & Validator Data

🥩

Staking

Lock HYPE to secure the network & earn rewards
How staking works: You delegate your HYPE to a validator who helps run the network. In return, you earn a share of new HYPE created each day (the APR). The more total HYPE staked network-wide, the lower the APR — but the more secure the chain becomes. You can unstake anytime, with a short cooldown period.
